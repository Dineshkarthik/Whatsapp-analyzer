How to export your Conversation:

To export a copy of the history of an individual chat or group, use the Email chat feature:
  • Open the conversation or group.
  • Click on more button (three vertial dots) on top right corner and select More.
  • Select Email chat.
  • Choose Without Media.
  • An email will be composed with your chat history attached as a .txt document.
  • Now send it to yourself or save it in drafts. Then download the .txt file and upload it below.
  • Before Uploading open the file and check the date format, whether Day comes first or the Month and Select an option below.
